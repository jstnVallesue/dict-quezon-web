What is iBPLS?

The iBPLS is a system developed by the Department of Information and Communications and Technology (DICT) to help LGUs streamline their systems and procedures. It also aims to expedite the processing of business permits.


BUSINESS PERMIT

The eBPLS (now iBPLS: Business Permit with Barangay Clearance – BPBC) is a cloud-based system that enables the LGUs and their constituents to apply for and process Business Permit and Barangay Clearance electronically.
